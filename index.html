Dispatch watches your schedule while this tab stays open: it pings a browser notification and chime 5 minutes before each program, then sounds a full alarm at start time with a one-tap call button. It can't place phone calls or send texts on its own — that needs a connected service like Twilio on a real server. Keep this tab open (or pinned) for reminders to fire.
